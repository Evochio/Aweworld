Admiral Avolc Game

You are Avolc, experienced captain of the east-sea Admiralty, the navy of the Awes.
As a player you will be guiding her actions and influencing her choices, help her become a better captain and leader or perhaps lead her to her doom.

Together you are about to face the challenges of a sea-captain during a crucial time in history where swathes of the world and seas remain unmapped and fledgeling aswell as old nations experience growth, modernization, social challenges and looming conflict.

Your choices will affect the outcome of the story and perhaps even the fate of the nations.

You will be measured in various stats, most of them hidden.
But there is Morale, Piety, injury, reputation with various characters, time and other secrets being measured from your choices that might lead you down different paths or unexpected ends.
