Admiral Avolc

Admirals log. Avolc

Safepouch gulf

Day 11

Its come to my attention we are down 1 Raptorian. Not to disease, accident or famine but to a game of cards and the belly of a Wulf. Sergeant Evoc reports that Logistics officer Keeper, has been engaging Raptorians in high stakes gambling and winning. Winning so much in fact that he won the rights to devour one of them. Raptorians being what they are of course accepted the loss.

Raptorians are an expensive resource and our best fighters for boarding actions. I will not tolerate unnecessary attrition to their numbers. I have however been strongly recommended against forbidding the games the crew play on their ofttimes by First-officer Arn, it apparently keeps them occupied to such a degree that it diverts them from other mischief. I suppose we’ve had an unusually low level of tension and attrition on this voyage so far, though im hesitant to acknowledge the games as the cause.

Still, i feel i have to decide whether to put a stop to this practice or at least put restrictions on it.
