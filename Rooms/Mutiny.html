Before you can dread the implications of that, attention is directed toward you. Arn steps forward, brows furrowed.

If that was an apology meant to placate you, you're not given long to think about it. They do not doom you to the sea. Instead the wide-open jaws of a Raptorian suddenly greet your view.

What better way to make a captain dissapear then the wide large belly of a large reptile like that. You are wolfed down with ease and abandoned to your thoughts in the dark cramped stomach. Until the churning and rumbling belly overtakes you.
