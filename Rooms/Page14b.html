Admiral Avolc

Admirals log. Avolc

I was approached by Sergeant Evoc today about the matter of the gambling going on below deck during the crews off-times. He reported we are now down additionally 2 Raptorians to the games of chance and debt leading them down into their debtors guts.

Evoc further went to inform that the games are causing a problem with the Raptorians, as boredom of long voyage and closed-in quarters causes them to seek excitement and thrills wherever they can get it. They are reckless gamblers with little thought of consequence, a strong trait for battle perhaps, but not so much in games of chance. 

Many are now bound down by debts and more of them risk being tempted into "All or nothing" kind of debts that might lead to doom or salvation.

This is troubling news.
