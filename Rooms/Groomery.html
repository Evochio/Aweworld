The Groomery

Im approached, shortly after seeing off the Kingdom Trade-cruiser, by none other then the boats resident Matron.

While the largest Awe aboard, she's remarkable subtle in her movements and I dont notice her presence straight away. Elda is the kind of Awe that puts the heart at ease, with her perpetual soft smile and demeanor of unquestionable sincerity.

As an Matron-Awe in-charge of the ships physical and mental health, I couldn't have asked for a better candidate.

“May I borrow you for a few moments, dear captain?” She coos softly. In truth, she is the sole officer who could in some cases overrule and even order me around. But as true to her calling, she's all manners.

“Certainly” I respond hesitantly. Officers seldom come to visit the captain with good news.

"Just thought it was time we got a little aquinted dear captain, straighten out some of those tangles. No need to be tense."

We make our way to the heart of the ship. “The Groomery” as its sometimes called is located right in the mid-level center of the ship. It allows fast access to it from most parts of the ship.

The namesake itself is self-explanatory. A healthy coat is a healthy Awe. When there are no general medical needs, the nurses there provide much needed assisted grooming. "Cleanly tangle-free fighters, or you invite the fluff-biters." as the saying goes.

Today its empty and im guided into a chair and the Matron herself sets to fussing over the tangles in my mane with a large brush.

“How have your first weeks been, captain?” She murmurs conversationally. I know what this is, its my check-up of sorts. A firm stroke of brush and claw does its job at putting me at ease and relax.
