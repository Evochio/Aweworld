You’re controlling Captain Avolc about the Awe Galley-Frigate 'Attentive' as she journeys to find the Awes Admiralty ‘3rd Fleet’ in the large winding archipelago of ‘Thousand Islands’. Instructions

Through Chapter 2, you will be given the option to select an officer to perform a task, often related to a problem or investigation.
The Captain is not supposed to get their paws dirty themselves unless it's really necessary, they have a crew for that.

Here is a quick example of stats you might see.

Martial - Represents a characters Combat and Tactical skills, or general physical prowess.
Diplomacy - Represents a characters Charm and Negotiating ability.
Technical - Represents a characters ability to perform complicated tasks or skill at their speciality.

These stats are mere guidelines to a characters overall competence or experience. A character with higher stats is not always guaranteed to perform better than one with less however.
The characters quirks or personality always takes precedent.
