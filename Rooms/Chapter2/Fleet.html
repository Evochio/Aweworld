At one point we stop at an Island briefly and a scout flies out onto the ‘Defiant’ and delivers a report, no doubt updating Captain Morlo on the fleets position.
Then off we sail again, to catch up to their location. It seems the fleet stays on constant the move.

‘Swift Bastard’ is amply named. The smaller ship is light and nimble and races ahead of us quite a deal many times. They always wait for us to catch up in the end however.
Officer Nyxie is taking this as a bit of an affront and is pushing our ship hard to go full speed. There are moments that we are matching their pace, but not for long periods, we’re just too heavy in the water with all our cargo.
It seems Captain Koen knows where we’re going. It’s a jumble of island clusters that would have taken us days to investigate, but we bypass it all. It’s a good thing we have a guide.

The Fleet

The fateful day has arrived. The look-out calls out first, spotting ships, many ships. Then I see them too, a score of sails coming our way with haste.
Much of the crew pause what they’re doing to have a look as we approach the 3rd Fleet of the Admiralty, 10 warships are visible of various make. Most of them are Galley-Frigates like ours, but dispersed around the fleet are 3 imposing battleships, nearly double our size and bristling with Ballistae.
There are 2 other ships visible too, though less armed and armored. One looks like an ordinary merchant ship, just like the one we encountered before the Sea of Fangs. The other one is of similar shape, but of distinct elegant Kingdom flair.
We’re finally here. Our objective to find them and get their report can finally be completed. Though I have mixed feelings about returning home after we’re done here.
